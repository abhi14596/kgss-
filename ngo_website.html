Our Journey Since 2016

In 2019, we began our noble mission with water camps and food donation drives. What started as a simple initiative has grown into a movement dedicated to serving humanity.

Our motto, "Manav Mein Hi Madhav Mile" (Finding the divine in humanity), guides everything we do. We believe that true service to God lies in serving our fellow human beings. When we help someone, comfort the distressed, or bring hope to the hopeless, we experience the presence of the divine in humanity itself.

Under the leadership of Kali Kumar Agarwal, son of the late Shri M.L. Agarwal, Krishna Gopal Seva Samithi has been consistently working to make this vision a reality. Our journey, which began with modest water distribution camps in 2014, has evolved into a comprehensive service organization touching countless lives.

Today, we continue this sacred mission with the same dedication and passion, believing that every act of kindness brings us closer to understanding the true essence of humanity and divinity.
