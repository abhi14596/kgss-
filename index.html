Our Journey Since 2016

In 2016, we began our noble mission with water camps and food donation drives. What started as a simple initiative has grown into a movement dedicated to serving humanity.

Our motto, "Manav Mein Hi Madhav Mile" (Finding the divine in humanity), guides everything we do. We believe that true service to God lies in serving our fellow human beings.

Under the leadership of Kapi; Kumar Agarwal, son of the late Shri M.L. Agarwal, Krishna Gopal Seva Samithi has been consistently working to make this vision a reality.

Today, we continue this sacred mission with dedication and passion, believing that every act of kindness brings us closer to understanding the true essence of humanity.
